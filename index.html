About YAC

The Youth Advancement Coalition was founded with insight and research that the STIGMA associated with mental health illness is further compounded by lack of awareness, resource availability and guidance for the vulnerable communities. Mental health illness ramifications include substance use and in turn negative outcomes and mortality.

We are devastated by the youth accretion in marginalized communities due to this mental health and substance use crisis and action is a necessity.
